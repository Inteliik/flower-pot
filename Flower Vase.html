Written instructions

  1. Get a plastic bottle.
  2. Cut off the top, triangular part of the bottle. Dont throw it away as you will need it later.
  3. From the bottle, cut off a not too thin ring.
  4. Keep it aside for now.
  5. Now, cut the bottle into strips from top but not fully all the way down.
  6. From the topp of each strip, bend a little bit of the plastic outwards towards yourself.
  7. Hold all the strips together and slide the ring you cut earlier in.
  8. Let go of the strips and turn the bottle upside-down and press down spreading out the strips you cut.
  9. Keep the bottle upside down and attach the bent part of the the strips to the ring.
  10. After all of them have been attached, make sure its secure and turn the bottle rightside-up again. you will see that it has taken the form of a pot.
  11. Now, take the triangular part of the bottle, take off the cap and place it upside-down inside the pot you have created.
  12. Voila, you Flower Vase is ready to have some flowers in it.
